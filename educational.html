Spot Leverage vs Duration Options

To trade effectively like a professional inside institutional liquidity feeds, you must master the two separate clearing frameworks provided on the trading desk.

1. Spot Risk Parameter Processing (SL/TP)

When you deploy a Spot order, your contract stays open indefinitely until price reaches your predefined safety boundaries. Stop Loss (SL) sets an exact line where the contract cuts losses automatically. Take Profit (TP) specifies the exact exit point to capture earnings before a reversal.

2. Fixed Duration Options Contract (Deriv Style)

A duration options model is a time-bound derivative contract. You stipulate the exact lifespan of your order. When the stopwatch timer counts down to zero, the engine compares the exit price against your entry target. If a Buy contract closes even a fraction of a pip above entry, you win a premium layout return. If it closes below, the collateral is completely cleared.
